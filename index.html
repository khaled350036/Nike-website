About Nike

Nike is an American multinational corporation specializing in the design, manufacturing, and marketing of footwear, apparel, and sports equipment. It was founded in 1964 by Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman under the name Blue Ribbon Sports before rebranding as Nike Inc. in 1971. Nike is famous for its iconic "Swoosh" logo and the slogan "Just Do It." As one of the world's largest sportswear brands, Nike provides innovative products for both professional athletes and casual sports enthusiasts. The company sponsors top athletes and teams worldwide, including Cristiano Ronaldo, LeBron James, and Kylian Mbappé. It also owns popular sub-brands such as Converse and Jordan Brand. Nike is committed to innovation in sportswear, utilizing advanced technologies like Nike Air, Flyknit, and React to enhance performance and comfort.
